PA Mis-sell - Top Refund Category

Program Advisors incorrectly sold programs - wrong course, double enrollment, or misrepresented features. Action: Audit call scripts, add post-enrollment confirmation emails, introduce peer review for new PAs.

Majority of Refunds in Trial Window

Most learners drop before completing the trial period. Action: Strengthen Day-1 SSA check-in calls, improve first-week onboarding, assign dedicated success advisors immediately after enrollment.

Flagship ML - Highest Refund Count

Highest refund count among all courses. Action: Cross-reference with enrollment numbers to assess refund rate. If disproportionate, investigate early-class quality and PA expectation-setting.

Content / Curriculum Issues

Learners found content too basic or misaligned with their goals. Action: Product team to review early-module depth in PM, TPM, and AI programs.

Financial Hardship - Significant Category

Visa uncertainty, employer sponsorship failing, unexpected debt. Action: Introduce proactive hardship pause policy before learner reaches refund stage.

Policy Exceptions - Track Accountability

Refunds approved outside normal policy signal ad-hoc decision making. Action: Define formal exception criteria with documented approver accountability.

Learners Unreachable at Onboarding

VM - 4 Attempts cases mean learners were never reached at onboarding. Action: Verify contact info at enrollment, use WhatsApp as backup, improve sales-to-OB handoff.

H1B Visa - Macro Risk for US Cohorts

Multiple US learners cited H1B policy changes as refund reason. Action: Communicate deferral flexibility proactively; add geo-specific clauses for US cohorts.
